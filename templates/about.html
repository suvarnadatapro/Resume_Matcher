About Resume Matcher

Resume Matcher helps recruiters and job seekers quickly evaluate how well resumes match one or more job descriptions. It combines traditional TF-IDF cosine similarity with modern sentence embeddings to produce meaningful scores.

  • TF-IDF Cosine Similarity
  • BERT-based Semantic Similarity
  • Average score per job description

Built with Python, Flask, and Sentence Transformers.
